Our Plan

Profit Sharing: We Grow Together

For Workers

The Annual Bonus Pool

Every year, 5% of the platform's total annual profit is set aside specifically for our workers. This is distributed among the Top 100 workers based on performance.

How You Rank

Rankings are calculated using our Performance Score:
• Star Ratings (20%)
• Reliability Score (30%)
• Job Completion Rate (20%)
• Volume of Work (10%)
• Earnings Volume (20%)

For Promoters

Monthly Payouts

We allocate 5% of monthly revenue to promoters. Payouts are calculated at the end of every month and distributed based on contribution scores.

Contribution Score

Your share depends on your impact:
• 1 Point per Waitlist Referral
• 3 Points per Worker Onboarded
• 2 Points per Employer Onboarded
Higher score = Higher share of the 5% pool.

Terms & Conditions

1. Verification: All workers must undergo Aadhaar verification and background checks before becoming eligible for the platform.

2. Direct Payments: MaidConnect guarantees 95% of service fees go directly to the worker via UPI. We retain only 5% for platform maintenance and profit pools.

3. Profit Eligibility: To be eligible for the Annual Worker Profit Share, a worker must have completed at least 50 jobs in the calendar year and maintained a rating of 4.0 stars or higher.

4. Promoter Payouts: Promoter payouts are processed on the 1st of every month. Minimum payout threshold is ₹500.

5. Dispute Resolution: All disputes regarding payments or service quality must be raised within 48 hours of job completion via the app. The Admin's decision is final.
