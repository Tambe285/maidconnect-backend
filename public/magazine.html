Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

🎯 What do I need to qualify for the Annual Profit Share?

Two simple requirements:
1️⃣ Complete at least 50 jobs in the calendar year (Jan 1 – Dec 31)
2️⃣ Maintain an average rating of 4.0 stars or higher from employers

Meet both, and you're automatically entered into the Top 100 ranking for profit distribution.

How much can I earn from the profit share?

Every year, 5% of MaidConnect's total annual profit is distributed among eligible workers. Top performers typically earn between ₹5,000 – ₹50,000 as a year-end bonus!

What counts as a "completed job"?

A job counts when:
✓ You finish the scheduled work
✓ The employer marks it "Complete" in the app
✓ Payment is processed via UPI

Cancelled, disputed, or no-show jobs do NOT count toward your 50-job goal.

What if I don't meet the criteria this year?

No stress! You still keep 95% of every job payment (more than any other platform). The profit share is just a bonus. Many workers start part-time and qualify the following year once they build their reputation.

When do I receive the profit share payment?

Calculations happen on January 15th each year. Payments are sent directly to your registered UPI within 7 business days.

Terms & Conditions

1. Verification: All workers must undergo Aadhaar verification and background checks before becoming eligible for the platform.

2. Direct Payments: MaidConnect guarantees 95% of service fees go directly to the worker via UPI. We retain only 5% for platform maintenance and profit pools.

3. Profit Eligibility: To be eligible for the Annual Worker Profit Share, a worker must have completed at least 50 jobs in the calendar year and maintained a rating of 4.0 stars or higher.

4. Promoter Payouts: Promoter payouts are processed on the 1st of every month. Minimum payout threshold is ₹500.

5. Dispute Resolution: All disputes regarding payments or service quality must be raised within 48 hours of job completion via the app. The Admin's decision is final.
