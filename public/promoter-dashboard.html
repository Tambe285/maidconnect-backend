💡 How Promoter Earnings Work

  • 1. Every month, 5% of subscription revenue goes into the Promoter Pool
  • 2. Your share = (Your onboarded businesses ÷ Total all promoters' onboarded)
  • 3. More businesses you onboard = bigger % of the pool
  • 4. Payouts happen on the 5th of next month
  • 5. Every 1st of the month, counters reset to ZERO - fresh start!
